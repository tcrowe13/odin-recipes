Lasagna Recipe

Description

"One of the oldest pasta dishes, it is made from wide flat pasta sheets and filled with various meats, vegetables and cheeses.

  • garlic
  • tomatoes
  • sugar
  • spices
  • lasagna noodles
  • cheeses
  • egg

Steps

  1. make the meat sauce
  2. cook the lasagna noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
